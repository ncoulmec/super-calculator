Illustrative only — general information, not financial advice. Your Gross Total Agreed Rate inc. super means just that — inclusive of super: the cash to your bank is that figure (excluding GST) divided by 1.12, and the super is the remainder. GST is yours to remit to the ATO via your BAS as normal. Genuine expense reimbursements (e.g. accommodation, parking with receipts) aren't included in earnings and aren't shown here.
